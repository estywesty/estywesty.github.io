About Me

With over 5 years of experience in the design industry and 3 as in-house designer, I can offer first hand experience in corporate branding through a variety of applications, such as print and digital advertising, collateral, user interface and experience, giveaways, and tradeshow stand design. I am also well versed in more unusual projects, such as office interior design, award ceremony backdrops and prizes, car wraps, and team-building memorabilia.

As a result, I not only have the ability to deliver great design work, but have the interpersonal skills required to successfully develop and implement them throughout a company and/or product.

I am not only passionate about effective and functional design, but remain an enthusiastic student to an ever evolving industry, inevitably bringing that passion to my work and those around me.
